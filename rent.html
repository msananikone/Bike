Strengths:
The Specialized 1999 FSR is a really solid and fun MTB, and for it's age is surprisingly light, The frame is good looking, and lasts forever.
Weakness:
The only weaknesses I could find on the bike is it's lackluster rear travel, however this is fixable by making a modified link, or stealing one off one designed for the downhill model, which allows for a lot more travel, You'll also need a disc brake adapter if you want to run them. This bike overall, with the right modifications can be an excellent trail bike. If you're willing to spend the money, then It's a good frame to convert into a DH/FR bike. A lot of fun.
